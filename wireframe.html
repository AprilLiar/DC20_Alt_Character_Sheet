Biography

Thalindra Ashveil grew up in the ancient forests of the Silverbark Reach, among a clan of high elves who served as wardens of the old growth. From childhood she showed an uncanny gift for reading the land — the way wind bent through grass before a predator moved, the silence that preceded ambush.

At the age of 112 she left the Reach after a human logging company backed by the Merchant's Guild razed the western grove. She still searches for the mastermind behind the contract.
